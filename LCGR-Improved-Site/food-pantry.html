Food Pantry!

Open every 1st and 3rd Friday of the month, 11am to 4pm.

*Photos are not reflective of current Pantry stock.

Our Food Pantry offers free, shelf-stable food to LGBTQ+ individuals (and our Allies!) in need.

​

The Pantry is open every 1st and 3rd Friday of the month from 12pm to 4pm. For exact dates, check our monthly calendar!

View Our Monthly Calendar!

The Pantry also has resources for applying for SNAP benefits, nutrition, recipes, and more! All provided generously by Helping Harvest.

​

We DO NOT require ID! We do however have some paperwork you will need to fill out. If you need assistance with these forms, please ask the Staff.
