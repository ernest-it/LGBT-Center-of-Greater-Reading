Queer Quest: Orange Game

EVERY 1ST THURSDAY, 6PM TO 8PM!

NEW ADVENTURES IN EACH GAME!

One-shot sessions -- short, self-contained adventures designed to introduce you to the game and each other. If there's enough interest and magic at the table, we can grow this into a continuing campaign where your characters' stories evolve from session to session!​

​

  • Ages 18+ only.

  • Come prepared! Bring your own laptops or writing tools.

  • Beginner friendly, everyone is welcome!

​

This game is hosted by Jose Joel (He/Him)!
