Our Services

Individual
Counseling

Meet one-on-one with a dedicated and skilled counselor. Here, we will address emotional, psychological, or behavioral issues to help you navigate your mental health.

Couples
Counseling

Having trouble navigating your relationships? We offer LGBTQ+ focused couples counseling to help you and your partner(s) navigate these difficult times!

Youth
Counseling

Our counseling is also available for youth! Whether it's navigating school, family, future plans, identity, or something else, our counselors are prepared to help the youth in our community grow into healthy adults.

Gender Affirming
Care Letters

We offer Gender Affirming Care Letters! Some doctors may require you to get a letter before starting Hormones Replacement Therapy (HRT) or receiving Gender Affirming Surgery. Our counseling services provide these letters!​​

LGBTQ+ Mental Health Statistics:
Why LGBTQ+ Focused Counseling Matters

These graphics provide statistics for LGBTQ+ individuals & their mental health in comparison to their non-LGBTQ+ peers.

​

LGBTQ+-focused counseling matters. LGBTQ+ individuals are far more likely than non-LGBTQ+ individuals to require counseling due to discrimination, abuse, and hate that they experience.
