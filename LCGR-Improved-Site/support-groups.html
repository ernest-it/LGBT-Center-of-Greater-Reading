Safe Space
Survivors Support Group

Support group for survivors of Sexual Assault / Intimate Partner Violence. Open to anyone of any identity or presentation.

 

Group is 100% anonymous. 18+ only.

For information: Clinical@lgbtcenterofreading.com

VOICES: LGBTQ+ Youth Group
(Vision, Opportunity, Inclusivity, Communication, Education, and Support)

Youth Support, Social, and Educational group! We help LGBTQ+ youth connect with their peers, learn life skills, and connect with their local community!

​

Ages 14 to 18. 18 if still in school.

Meets every Thursday from 5:00pm to 6:30 pm.

Downloadable Flyer PDF file HERE.

H.E.R - LGBTQ+ Women's Group
(Harmony, Empathy, and Rejoicing)

Our support/social group for LGBTQ+ women all across the rainbow! We welcome LGBTQ+ women to discuss their unique experiences and struggles while connecting with their community.

​

18+ only.

Meets every second Friday from 6pm to 8pm.

Downloadable Flyer PDF file HERE.

Rainbow Alliance:
LGBTQ+ Spiritual Support Group

Spiritual support group open to anyone of any spiritual background. Ages 18+ only.

Meets every 4th Tuesday monthly from 7pm to 8pm
