"How Do I Become A Volunteer?"

It takes three simple steps!

Step 1: Fill Out The Application

Our Volunteer Application helps us get a feel for you, your interests, and your experience! Fill out the application by scanning the QR code or clicking this link: https://forms.gle/rQGAW7UUsEDR2ckY7

​

Our Volunteer Coordinator, Jox, will reach out to you within a week of the application being submitted!

​

NOTE: We do allow individuals under 18 to volunteer, but written and signed parental/guardian permission is required. Certain volunteer opportunities are 18+ only.

Step 2: Complete Your Background Checks

We require three background checks and a mandatory reporting training to be completed before you can begin volunteering. These include:

​

1) PA Child Abuse History Clearance

2) PA Criminal History Clearance [Alt Link]

3) FBi Finger Printing - NOTE: FBI Fingerprinting is NOT required if you have lived in Pennsylvania for 7+ years. Email Jox for an Identigo fingerprinting code..

4) Mandatory Reporting Training - 2-3 hour class on how to recognize and report the signs of child abuse.

 

Completed clearances must be submitted to our Event/Volunteer Coordinator, Jox.​

​​

NOTICE: The LGBT Center cannot cover any fees associated with background checks.

Step 3: Volunteer Interview

We want to get to know you before you join our team! Reach out to Jox to schedule a volunteer interview and get a tour of our center!
