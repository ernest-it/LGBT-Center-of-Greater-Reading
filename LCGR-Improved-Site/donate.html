DONATE TODAY!

Your donations support our mission to provide advocacy, support, and education to the LGBTQ+ communitiy in Berks County! But where do your donations go, exactly? Well, for example:
 

  • $10 - Helps us provide games, books, and more to our visitors and groups

  • $25 - Helps fund our Youth Group for one week

  • $50 - Covers counseling for one individual per week

  • $100 - Provides transportation services for our Youth Group for one month

  • $250 - Helps us acquire supplies for our community outreach events

  • $500 - Helps fund our community education programs

  • $1000 - Provides funding for our various support groups and social groups for one month

More Ways To Give

DAF Direct

DAF Direct allows you to combine the most favorable tax benefits with the flexibility to support your favorite causes! It primarily allows you to recommend grants directly to our Center. Use the widget here to do so!

Dedications

Is there someone you'd like to donate in the honor/memory of? Include "In honor/memory of [individual(s)'s name]" in the notes section of our form!

Employer Match

You could double the impact of your gift today! Will your employer match your donation? List your employer in the notes section of our form and we'll keep an eye out for their match!

Legacy Gifts

You can choose to leave a part of your estate to The LGBT Center of Greater Reading. You can leave behind a legacy of giving and become a permanent piece of the Center's history!

Amazon Wishlist

Want to support a specific program? Our Amazon Wishlist includes supplies for our Counseling programs, general operations, groups, and more! Simply choose from our list what you would like to give and it'll be on it's way!
