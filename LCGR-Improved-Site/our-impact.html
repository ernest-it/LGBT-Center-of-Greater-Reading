The People We Impact Are Real,
And Their Stories Matter.

Listen to these stories from real clients, visitors, and Staff members here at The Center.

Gary's Story

"I was lost, I had no place to go. I wanted a chance, and then, coming here...I'm making myself better because I have people who believe in me.

Eva's Story

"As soon as I walked in here, they accepted me for who I was immediately. It was one of the best feelings I've ever had.

Jox's Story

Community Engagement Specialist
at The LGBT Center

"Being here at The Center is the first time I've never had to mask my Autism...This place is very accepting of people like us."

Josh's Story

"I keep coming back for the deep sense of comradery I feel when I come here...It's really wonderful to come here and find people who are here to celebrate each other and be themselves."

Susan's Story

Sexual Assault Navigator

Laura's Story
